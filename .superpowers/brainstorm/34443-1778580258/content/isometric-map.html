The Battlefield — Isometric Overworld

The Concept

An isometric overworld map — each topic is a location (castle, village, tower) connected by dotted paths. Healthy topics are green and thriving. As you drag chaos from the arsenal sidebar, buildings crumble, catch fire, or get swallowed by lava. The map tells the story of your cluster's state at a glance.
