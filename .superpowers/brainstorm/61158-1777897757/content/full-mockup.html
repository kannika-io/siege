Isometric grid with all castle tiers and ruins

The Kingdom — Siege Console

Design Details

  • Grid: Isometric diamond grid on green terrain. Empty slots shown as dashed outlines.
  • Castles: 3 SVG tiers (tower, castle, fortress) + ruins state. Gold labels beneath each.
  • Interaction: Click a castle to open the existing detail panel (slide-in from right).
  • Placement: Topics fill next available slot organically. Position stored by topic name.
  • Destruction: Deleted topic → ruins sprite stays on tile. Same-name topic rebuilds there.
  • Real-time: SSE events animate new castles appearing and destroyed ones becoming ruins.
